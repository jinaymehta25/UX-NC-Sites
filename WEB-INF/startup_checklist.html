Business Startup Planning Checklist

We've created a three-step outline to help you with information to get started. The requirements vary according to your business activity.

1. Outline a business plan

Not sure if you have a viable idea for a business? Need to map out your strategy? Get ahead of the game and plan your business.

2. Meet State Requirements

  • Decide on your business structure and register your business name.
  • Identify license requirements.
  • Prepare to pay your tax obligations. Register with the N.C. Department of Revenue if needed.
  • Fulfill employer responsibilities and meet employer reporting requirements

3. Learn about available resources
